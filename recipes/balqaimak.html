Balqaimaq recipe

Description

Balqaimaq (bal- honey, qaimaq - sour cream) is a traditional Kazakh treat made by mixing rich, thick qaimaq (clotted cream) with honey, creating a sweet,
spread often enjoyed with bread or as a dessert. Traditionally offered to honored guests in Kazakh homes as a sign of respect and
hospitality. Represents the pastoral heritage of the Qazaq nomadic lifestyle, where dairy foods play an important role.

Ingridients

  • Sour cream 25% - 400 g
  • Honey - 180 g
  • Milk - 70 ml
  • Flour - 30 g

Steps

  1. Pour the sour cream into a saucepan and place it over a water bath for about 6-7 minutes.
  2. Then add the honey and whisk until smooth.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the milk and flour, stirring well so there are no lumps.
  4. Next, slowly pour the milk-flour mixture into the saucepan with the sour cream and honey, whisking quickly and thoroughly.
